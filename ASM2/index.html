PROJECT DESCRIPTION

4-TECH TEAM

Overview:

Topic

Since technology is a field that is growing exponentially over time, it is easy that newcomers can get confused with specific majors. Thus, the focus of our attention is going to make a website for online learning. The expected outcome of our website is going to share general knowledge in the IT fields. Later on, specific skills and how to enter the profession in a particular major without making any confusion will be introduced. We name that project with a “4-Tech Academic Coding Website" and the website name is "4Tech", which has several meanings. Firstly, "4" represents four members of this first project followed with "Technology". It also means implicit in "for technology", which is all our effort for technology.

In order to achieve such things, having a strong knowledge of each particular field is a must. Initially, the general picture of this project heavily depends on the methodology of teaching, which means that a good programmer may not teach very well. Therefore, the main challenge of this project is how to validate that knowledge and demonstrate it in a friendly way. As a result, imparting knowledge efficiency and avoiding as many academic theories is vital to let beginners find interest in the course and later in their career paths. To implement those work successfully, consulting with professionals in our school and the advice of them is an essential part of the success of this project.

Motivation

The motivation we have mainly from what we have experienced as a beginner. Enrolling in the field that contains too many subfields and the difficulty of its applications in the real-world made us come up with the idea of online teaching. Our project not only assists people to learn new things but also reduces the time-consume of getting to a specific place to learn. Tired of getting around will result in the efficiency of performance and produce less effectiveness in obtaining new knowledge. Therefore, students may feel free to control their schedules and activities and be more open with the course they enrolled in. Also, this project can help us form a solid knowledge base during teaching. To be precise, teaching is also a form of learning and reminds us about previous experience and skills we have passed.

Landscape

The same idea of our projects is the Udemy website where people can purchase and join those courses. The courses in Udemy are various categories, in which the contributors are users. That platform has gained an enormous reputation for the success of learning online where their aim is professional adults and students, which means they have a wide range of customers. From the customer's standpoint, this platform is much more reliable compared to our website. However, because of the variety of categories and a bunch of courses and contributors, it is less likely that the quality of those courses can be good; therefore, our website will upgrade this point to meet customer’s desires.

Our website has many outstanding features that can attract newcomers and engage them in the course. Firstly, we provide a clear roadmap and a good vision for each online course that can let the pupil have a general view of what they are going to deal with and later in the future when they have finished this course. This action will keep them not confused and not get disoriented when they obtain new things. Secondly, providing tasks and questions after finishing each part of the courses may show their flaw in learning and let them revise the course again. Also, after they finish their course, there will be a small project for practicing and implementing some practical applications. It can be some projects that we have completed or some plans that others have done. This kind of work will make them engage and perform well later in the company. To end up, having a certificate of finishing this course, which is validated by some prestigious and quality organizations to ensure information and knowledge is adequate.

In the age of technology, where more and more people want to learn more about the industry and to get their best in understanding it. During their process, they could head into some problems in their beginning stage. The aim of this project is to help new learners from all ages in the IT industry, coding to be specific, to achieve the basic knowledge of the coding language of their choice (Python, JAVA, HTML, etc.) and to create a platform that could help them strengthen their understanding in the language. Our aim for this project comes from our difficulties in learning experiences in the course, so we would want to help create something that could reduce the difficulties that most new learners can possibly come across.

Our goal for this project is to have a website that has a basic knowledge of coding languages (Python, HTML, JAVA, etc.). The content will be checked and feedback by lecturers and students who achieved high achievements in the course, this will help increase the validity of our information in this section.

3. Content Team - Building the content

a. Academic


To prepare for the Academic section, we have to spend most of our time studying and determining the knowledge exceptionally thoroughly. We are meticulous in selecting the expertise and content in order to convey to the students. The Academic section is constructed up of small areas like Lectures, Video Tutoring, Exercise, and Specialized Vocabulary. The process of integrating the content of each division will be exhibited below:
- Lectures: There are a lot of websites that teach programming in the form of lectures - all text. That makes it straightforward for apprentices to apprehend and keep up with the course in the way of documentation. 4 - Tech will also build the lesson partly in words. Still, it will be as clear, brief, and concise as possible, and obligation is complete and accurate. We have organized this content with our knowledge and at the same time refer to several platforms currently on the market such as Leetcode, W3school, Kaggle.

- Video tutoring: Identifying the necessary between lectures and visual instructions assist students in absorbing and retaining information for a longer time. Risks

Wrong Content:
Detail: The content of the course or each video can be inaccurate and result in the quality of the program.

Cause: Since our team still studies in university, solid knowledge in a particular field of IT is nearly impossible, which can result in the wrong content.

Solution: Collaborate with some prestigious or quality organizations to validate those content.


Outdated content:
Detail: Since providing online learning are mainly using once time video. The videos can be outdated.

Cause: Information technology is a field that changes swiftly, outdated technology can be explained.

Solution: Updating new content every 2 years.


Copyright video:
Detail: The content of the video can be stolen from another website and users for their aims, which could be damaging to our commercial.

Cause: The content of our videos might better than others and attract many students and professionals.

Solution: Have copyright for each video and do not allow to download a video in order to minimize the risk of leaking it.


Display issue:
Detail: The video itself can be lost due to the website issue and result in the user's experience.

Cause: Not having a robust system and maintenance of code can result in hardly debugging code.

Solution: Have to build a strong system of the website to handle a vast amount of traffic of users.


Security issue:
Detail: Because the customer has to provide their identity to the website so that we can validate the user, private information can be lost by the hacker.

Cause: An attack of hackers can hugely damage our awareness and reputation of security.

Solution: Have some policy about our product and hire some professionals about security.
